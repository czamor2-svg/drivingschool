Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of driving techniques do you teach? +
At Bally's Auto School, we instruct about many different techniques at no additional charge. These include defensive driving methods, fuel-saving techniques, highway driving, and urban navigation. We take safety seriously and pride ourselves on teaching you the safest driving techniques to get the results you want.
Can you evaluate my current driving skills? +
Yes. We offer company driver evaluations and will bring you up to date on the newest laws and techniques. Whether you are a new driver or an experienced one looking to sharpen your skills, we can assess where you are and build a plan from there.
Do you offer online courses? +
Yes. We offer the NYS-required 5-hour pre-licensing course entirely online. You can complete it at your own pace from any device. The course is fully NYS approved and accepted at all DMV locations statewide.
Do you offer sample DMV permit tests? +
Yes. We provide sample tests to help students prepare for their DMV permit exam. Our team is also available to assist with DMV paperwork and all licensing requirements to make the process as smooth as possible.
